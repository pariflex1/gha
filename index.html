About GHA Construction

Premier Civil Engineering & Infrastructure Firm in Jhansi

GHA Construction is a premier construction and civil engineering firm dedicated to transforming urban and rural landscapes through high-quality infrastructure. We have executed prestigious projects for the Baidyanath Group, Smart City Mission Jhansi, Satyashanti Group, and the Rural Engineering Department (RED).

From heavy-duty industrial utilities and city-level road networks to Smart City hospitals, residential complexes, and government office buildings — we deliver end-to-end engineering excellence that stands the test of time.
