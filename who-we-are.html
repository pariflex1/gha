Company Profile

Building the Future Since 2011

GHA Construction was founded with a vision to transform the construction landscape through innovation, quality, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small firm to one of the most trusted names in construction and infrastructure.

We specialize in civil construction, interior solutions, turnkey projects, housing developments, and project management consultancy, serving clients across residential, commercial, industrial, and government sectors.
